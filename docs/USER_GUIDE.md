# User Guide

## Employee

Sign in, review balances on Overview, and select **Apply leave**. Choose a leave type and dates; weekends and published holidays are excluded. Half-day is restricted to one date. Emergency mode permits a past start date. Use Leave History to inspect the three-stage timeline or cancel an eligible request.

## Reporting and Reviewing Officers

The dashboard shows only requests assigned to the signed-in officer (legacy unassigned requests remain visible). Open a request, review employee details, dates, reason and prior remarks, then recommend or reject with a clear remark.

## Approving Authority

The final desk shows requests recommended by both earlier stages. Final approval deducts the employee balance atomically. A future approved leave may be cancelled by the employee and is restored automatically.

## Administrator

- **Employees:** create/edit accounts, assign roles and the three-person approval chain, activate/deactivate, reset passwords or delete.
- **Leave records:** search by employee and filter by department, type and status.
- **Reports:** filter by date/department/type; export CSV for Excel or print to PDF.
- **Holidays:** publish dates excluded from working-day calculation.

## Status meanings

- Pending reporting: waiting for first-level action
- Pending review: Reporting Officer recommended
- Pending approval: Reviewing Officer recommended
- Approved: final approval complete and balance deducted
- Rejected: stopped at one approval stage
- Cancelled: withdrawn by employee
